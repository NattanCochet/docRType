Detailed Description

Represents the velocity of an object, defined by its speed, time duration, and coefficient.

The Velocity class encapsulates the velocity of an object, which consists of its speed (as a 2D vector), the time duration over which the velocity is applied, and a coefficient that might represent any scaling factor such as friction or resistance.
